What is TraqStarz?

Our app is an Android-based application aimed at people who enjoy motorbikes and going to tracks. Our application uses GPS tracking and telemetry data gathering to show statistics of a user's session while they are at a racetrack. Our users will have access to features such as map view, allowing them to place markers on the map overlay and create their own tracks using our application. Personal statistics such as top speeds on tracks, track completions and lap times will also be tracked and displayed to the user when finishing a session, saving to their personal profile afterwards.
