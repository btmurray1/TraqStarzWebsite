About us

Started in 2022, a group of young people passionate about motorbikes and coding wanted to create something to further inspire the racing community. We met whilst studying at Uni and became close friends and decided to collaborate as business partners. Each of us had a. interest in different areas that the application would have, so it was a perfect startup.

Our motivation came from a passion for racing and competitiveness and a broad interest social and community. This application does not is not aiming to solve a problem; instead, we aim to provide motorbike enthusiasts with a way to enhance their experience on the track.

Future application potentials can utilise AI for our track verification, estimated track statistics and allowing for the opt-in automation of sharing statistics to another profile that a user interacts with regularly.
