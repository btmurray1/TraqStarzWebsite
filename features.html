Subscription Features

Traqstarz comes with all the features you need to have a great time on the track without worrying about relying on manual timers or other sub standard applications.

For a subscription fee of $10 per month, all available features and future additions are yours to use as you please, no restrictions.
Lap timing
No better way to show your improvement than accurate timing of laps to see how well you're going.
Speed and acceleration data
Want to know your top speed? Average speed? Acceleration at any point on the track? This data and more is available for your viewing and comparison.
Track Libraries
With over 100 verified tracks and location based recommendations, its as easy as picking a track, then hitting record.
Map viewing and editing
Viewing maps and utilising the ability to customise tracks and pins allows you to use the maps best for you.
Friends statistics
Connect with old friends and make new ones. Compare statistics and compete with consistent updated profiles.
Forums
Communicate with all users of Traqstarz by accessing the forums. Questions, advice, sales, testimonials, all yours to post and read.
