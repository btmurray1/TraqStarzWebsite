About us

Started in 2022, A group of young passionate people about IT who wanted to create something and did. They all met whilst studying at Uni and became close friends and decided to collaborate as business partners.

What is Traqstarz?

Traqstarz is an Android-based application aimed at people who enjoy motorbikes and going to tracks, our app uses GPS tracking and telemetry data gathering to show statistics of a user's session while they are at a racetrack, our features such as map view, map tracking, personal statistics including top speeds, track completions and lap times.

Our Mission?

To offer a more modern, cleaner GUI with a more user-friendly layout than the existing applications, we want to create a safe community for like minded people who have a passion for racing and make sure they have fun doing it!

About you

We are looking for a security analyst with 2-3 years of experience in the field of cyber security. The perfect candidate would be someone who has proven experience in Security Incident Response, IAM, SIEMs and Understanding of databases (MS SQL Server, MySQL).

Responsibilities

- Use defence tools for monitoring and analysis of system activity to identify attacks.
- Act as primary contact for regular technical changes, this includes assisting business and technical teams regarding cybersecurity issues by supporting and assessing third-party technology solutions
- Install, maintain, and update security software
- System testing for vulnerabilities
- Develop Systems for the best security practices

Skills needed

- Proven knowledge of computer networks and common protocols: TCP/IP, UDP, DNS, FTP, SSH, SSL/TLS, HTTP
- Proficiency in one or more programming/scripting languages
- Excellent problem solving skills combined with hands-on experience doing root cause analysis and post incident reviews
- Demonstrated experience in Information Security with focus on Incident Response, Security Engineering, and/or Intrusion Detection

Qualifications

- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or equivalent degree
- CISA
- CISM
- CISSP
- IRAP (or similar)
